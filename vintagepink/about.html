About us

We are one of Portland's top vintage stores located in the popular Hawthorne District. With over 20 vendors in our 6,000 sq. ft of space you're sure to find some fantastic treasures. We specialize in mid-century pieces and also have an array of vintage, retro, industrial, reclaimed, vintage glassware, lighting and local one of a kind pieces!
